About Me

Posted on 2024-01-01 Edited on 2024-05-01

Hello World,

This is Di, living in my 25th year of life. I’m INTJ.

It’s been half a year since I graduated from school and started my career. School life and work life are drastically different but both of them are fascinating. I have changed a lot after school in many things such as habit and mindset. But one habit never changes is daily reading and learning. (Here are some of my pre-25-year-old notes: https://jokerdii.github.io/)

I still remember at the time when ChatGPT released I was taking “Quantitative Methods in NLP” course at MIT. Then I observed AI waves got stronger in 2023. Technology changed rapidly throughout the year with one great paper followed by another. And human’s life started to change rapidly as well. I don’t know whether it is because a lot of things going on outside, my mind is excited and spinning with full of information every single day.

I never had a habit of taking diary in my life. I didn’t film my life and I use social media only for chatting and “friend making”. But I finally decided to start it specifically for documenting what I read and learned in my spare time. And it is for me internal use only because 1) I’d like to write something as an output to make sure my brain exactly gets the input, and 2) I’d like to document great things because my brain has a very limited memory.
